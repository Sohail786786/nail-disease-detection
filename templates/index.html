How It Works

Our system uses advanced AI techniques to analyze nail images and detect common nail diseases at an early stage. Simply upload a clear image of your nail, and our trained models will provide a prediction along with the probability of each condition. This helps users take timely medical action.

Models Used in This Project

VGG16

Simple and effective convolutional network. Serves as a strong baseline for image classification in nail detection.

ResNet50

Uses residual learning to improve accuracy by enabling very deep networks without vanishing gradients.

InceptionV3

Efficient multi-scale feature extraction, capturing different sized patterns in nail images effectively.

Xception

Depthwise separable convolutions allow high accuracy with fewer parameters, ideal for complex image tasks.
